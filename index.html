About Us

Craftsmanship That Speaks For Itself

Ultra Marble & Stone Restoration is a trusted provider of natural stone care, known for delivering high-quality restoration services with precision, craftsmanship, and integrity.

Founded and led by Pedro Santiago, the company brings a hands-on, detail-focused approach to every project. Each surface is treated with care and technical expertise, resulting in consistent, high-quality results and finishes designed to last.

What We Do

Our Services

Professional restoration services for all types of natural stone surfaces.

Natural Stone Restoration

Restoration of marble, travertine, limestone, terrazzo, granite, onyx, and other natural stone surfaces. Services include professional refinishing to remove scratches and etches, improving overall appearance and restoring the stone to the desired finish. Finish options are available in matte, satin, or polished.

Learn More

Etch, Scratch & Wear Removal

Acid etching, surface scratches, and general wear are carefully corrected using proven restoration techniques. Rather than concealing imperfections, we properly resurface the stone to restore balance, uniformity, and clarity. This service is ideal for high-traffic areas and surfaces requiring precise correction.

Learn More

Stone Sealing & Protection

To preserve restored surfaces, we apply high-quality sealers that protect against moisture intrusion, staining, and daily wear. Proper sealing enhances longevity while maintaining the stone's natural appearance and finish.

Learn More

Shower Restoration

Restoration of shower surfaces including both tile and natural stone. Tile surfaces are professionally cleaned and sealed, while natural stone surfaces are cleaned, refinished to the desired finish, and sealed. This process improves appearance, cleanliness, and overall condition.

Learn More

Grout Care

Includes deep cleaning, re-grouting, mold and mildew removal, and sealing, for long-lasting results.

Learn More

Pool Deck Resurfacing

We resurface pool decks to improve traction, durability, and visual consistency. This process creates a safer outdoor surface while elevating the overall appearance of the space.

Learn More

We proudly serve residential, commercial, and marine properties, providing restoration solutions that protect, enhance, and preserve the natural beauty of fine stone surfaces.
